ABOUT

Old Dominion Honor Flight is a 501c3 nonprofit organization which “Honors American Veterans” for all their sacrifices. We transport these heroes to Washington, D.C. to visit their memorials at no cost to them. We are a local hub of the National Honor Flight Network, which consists of over 100 independent non-profit “hubs” across America.
Our coverage area basically runs from Elizabeth City, NC up to Fredericksburg, VA (including the Eastern Shore) and west to Farmville, VA. We invite all Veterans who served from Dec Apr to contact us to experience the meaning of "Honor Flight magic".
